Use the `artist-research` skill.

Research emerging artists suitable for support slots around a headliner.

Target profile:
- same broad scene as the headliner
- credible local or regional following
- active live identity or DJ profile
- reachable without major agency friction

Deliver:
- a ranked shortlist
- practical contact routes
- one-line notes on why each act makes sense on the bill

Be selective and reject names that are only algorithmically adjacent but not culturally aligned.
