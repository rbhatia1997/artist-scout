# Source Validation

Use source-backed verification. Prefer primary pages over hearsay.

## Preferred Sources

- artist SoundCloud pages
- artist Instagram bios
- Bandcamp artist pages
- artist Linktree or official site
- artist Spotify profile when public metadata is useful
- label compilation pages only when they clearly identify the artist

## Verification Rules

- confirm the page belongs to an artist, not a label or repost account
- use public profile text for contact and representation status
- estimate followers from public pages only
- if a fact is not visible, write `unclear`
- keep source links for every row

## Contact Rules

Valid public contact routes include:

- visible email
- public contact form
- clearly listed Instagram handle for DMs
- Bandcamp contact route
- booking or inquiry email

Do not fabricate contact info from guesses or third-party databases.

## Representation Rules

Use only these values unless the user overrides them:

- `none found`
- `likely independent`
- `management found`
- `unclear`

Interpretation:

- `management found`: page explicitly shows management, booking agency, or representation
- `likely independent`: direct self-contact and no visible management
- `none found`: no management evidence found, but independence is not strongly signaled
- `unclear`: source surface is too thin to call
